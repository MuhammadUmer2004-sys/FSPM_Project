# Freelance-Flow: Final Sprint Presentation

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Project:** Freelance-Flow (Option C)
**Course:** Fundamentals of Software Project Management
**Team Size:** 5 Members
**Objective:** Deliver a full Project Management Plan (PMP) and a functional MVP within 21 days.

---

## Slide 2: Project Overview & Scope
**What is Freelance-Flow?**
A dedicated bidding platform connecting clients who have specific software tasks with developers and agencies willing to bid on those tasks.

**Scope Boundaries:**
✔️ **In-Scope:** Simple User Authentication, Task Posting, Bid Submission, Basic Dashboard.
❌ **Out-of-Scope:** Payment Gateways, Escrow Services, Real-time Chat, Dispute Resolution.
*By clearly demarcating what is out-of-scope, we mitigate the biggest risk of software projects: scope creep.*

---

## Slide 3: Work Structure (WBS)
We utilized a multi-tier Work Breakdown Structure to manage this 3-week sprint efficiently:

1. **Initiation:** Requirements Gathering & Scope Definition.
2. **Design:** UI/UX Mockups & Application Architecture.
3. **Execution:** Frontend Authentication, Task Module, Bidding Module.
4. **Testing:** Unit Testing & Smoke Testing.
5. **Closure:** Final Presentation & Deployment.

---

## Slide 4: Budget & Cost Estimation
To ensure financial feasibility, we employed a Bottom-Up Estimation approach.

- **Direct Labor (`$14,300`):** Front-end, Architecture, QA, Project Management based on standardized hourly rates.
- **Infrastructure (`$100`):** Basic hosting and mock databases.
- **Contingency (`$2,160`):** A mandatory 15% reserve for identified and unidentified risks.
- **Total Project Budget:** `$16,560`

*Our estimates align perfectly with the allocated schedule of 21 days for 5 members.*

---

## Slide 5: Risk Management
We utilized a standard Risk Register matrix (Probability x Impact).
**Key Identified Risks:**
1. **API/Mock Failures (Score 16):** Mitigated by local storage fallbacks and extensive mock data seeds.
2. **Scope Creep regarding payments (Score 15):** Mitigated by having a strict sign-off on the scope document (payments explicitly out).
3. **Timeline constraint of 21 Days (Score 20):** Mitigated through rigorous agile daily standups and prioritizing MVP core features above all visual polish.

---

## Slide 6: Functional Prototype (Summary of Demo)
**Key MVP Mechanics Demonstrated:**
- **Client Flow:** A client logs in, fills out a form specifying task requirements, budget, and deadline, and posts it.
- **Developer Flow:** A developer browses available tasks, evaluates requirements, and submits a proposed bid amount.
- **Validation:** Our smoke testing phase guarantees that these two primary flows operate seamlessly without crashing.

**Conclusion:** Freelance-Flow proves that applying rigorous structured planning (WBS & AoN) significantly stabilizes rapid software delivery.
